# MyInteractions
Problem:Cora struggles to maintain social connections during busy days and often does not know what small action to take to stay in touch with others.

Solution:The MyInteraction app suggests simple social "sparks" based on the time of the day(Morning,Afternoon,Evening,etc.)helping Cora easily stay connected.Cora can open the app,enter or select the current time of day,and follow the suggested action.
